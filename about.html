About The Tech Brief

The Tech Brief is an independent technology publication created to simplify the fast-moving world of innovation. The platform covers AI, enterprise systems, cybersecurity, broadcast technology, consumer gadgets, startups, electric vehicles, and the tools shaping the future of work and digital life. Every article is written with clarity, accuracy, and real-world context so readers understand not just what is happening in tech, but why it matters.

Who I Am

My name is Prerak Mehta, and I have spent more than 25 years working in the IT industry, supporting large-scale systems, broadcast environments, and enterprise technology operations. My career has been built around solving complex technical problems, evaluating new tools, and helping teams adopt technology that actually works in real-world conditions.

Alongside my professional work, I run a YouTube channel called Prerak’s Tech World, where I review gadgets, explore new technologies, and break down industry trends for everyday users and tech enthusiasts. The same approach — clear, practical, and experience-driven — guides the content on this website.

Editorial Approach

Every article published on The Tech Brief is written by our editorial team as original analysis and commentary. We cover technology developments across AI, enterprise tech, cybersecurity, mobile, gaming, EVs, and more — applying editorial judgment to explain not just what is happening, but what it means for industry and consumers. Our coverage is independent, commercially unbiased, and updated continuously.
